### 🏠 What is Pinkhouse?
Pinkhouse is the operating system for **HOAs, Gated Villages, Condos, Townhouses, Apartments, Dorms, and Commercial Leasing**. By eliminating "Cloud Bloat" through the Flyover Stack, we provide world-class management tools without passing high infrastructure costs to our communities.
